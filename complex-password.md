
RapidReconciler has an option to enforce a complex password policy:
•	8 characters minimum length
•	Cannot contain the user's account name or parts of the user's full name that exceed two consecutive characters
•	Not be the same as the last 10 passwords
•	Must contain characters from three of the following four categories:
•	English uppercase characters (A through Z)
•	English lowercase characters (a through z)
•	Base 10 digits (0 through 9)
•	Non-alphabetic characters (e.g.  !, $, #, %)
•	Must be changed every 90 days
•	Must not be displayed, stored, or transmitted in clear text

LOGGING IN
When the user attempts to log in, if:
1.	90 days have elapsed since the last change
2.	They click on the “Forgot your password?” link and the link in the email sent to them then they will be brought to the “Password Reset” screen.
 
RESETTING THE PASSWORD
Once on this screen, the new password must conform to the policy. If the new password is less than 8 characters, then the confirm button remains disabled.
