About This Report
In-Transit Orders represent inventory shipped from one branch but not yet received at the destination. Until both sides post, the inventory balance is split across sending and receiving GL accounts. All 796 lines require matching receipts before period close to eliminate the in-transit variance in RapidReconciler. Branch names anonymized for display.
